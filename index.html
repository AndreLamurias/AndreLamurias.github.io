About

I'm an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science of NOVA School of Science and Technology, Lisbon, Portugal.

In the past I've worked at the Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University, as a post-doc research in the DKWE group, and at Priberam Labs.

I did my PhD at LASIGE, Faculdade de Ciencias, Universidade de Lisboa.

Research

My research work is mainly focused on information extraction applied to biomedical data, such as scientific papers, electronic health records and genomics. I work with machine learning algorithms and integrate the extracted information with domain ontologies.

On my GitHub page you can find past projects and projects I'm currently working on. My publications can be found on Google Scholar and Research Gate.
