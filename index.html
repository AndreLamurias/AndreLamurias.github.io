About

I'm a post-doc researcher at Aalborg University, Department of Computer Science, Database and Web Technologies group. My supervisors are Katja Hose and Thomas D. Nielsen.

In the past I've worked at Priberam Labs, and did my PhD at LASIGE, Faculdade de Ciencias, Universidade de Lisboa.

Research

My research work is mainly focused on data mining applied to biomedical data, such as scientific papers, electronic health records and genomics. I apply machine learning algorithms and integrate the extracted information with domain ontologies.

Projects

On my GitHub page you can find past projects and projects I'm currently working on.
